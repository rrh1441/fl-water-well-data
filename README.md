# Florida Water Well Data — County Statistics

County-level summary statistics for documented water wells in Florida, computed from the
official FL WMD/county well database: median drilled depth, static water level, tested yield,
and modeled drilling cost per county. See [`fl-water-well-county-statistics.csv`](./fl-water-well-county-statistics.csv).

Compiled and maintained by **[Florida Well Data](https://floridawelldata.com)** — free per-address well lookups and
full methodology at [floridawelldata.com](https://floridawelldata.com). Licensed CC-BY-4.0. Aggregate statistics only;
individual well records are not included.
